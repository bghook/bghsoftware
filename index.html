    Hello! I'm a dedicated and passionate Software Engineer, currently honing my full-stack development skills at Booz Allen Hamilton since January 2023. My journey in this fast-paced tech world began at the University of Georgia, where I graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science in 2019. My academic curiosity previously led me to the University of Alabama where I explored the realms of Political Science, earning both my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees.

    My professional path has been diverse and enriching. Before joining Booz Allen Hamilton, I contributed to Lockheed Martin's ambitious projects, where I played a pivotal role in developing Python software for missile flight programs and automating crucial processes. My tenure at SAIC saw me diving deep into C++ for onboard flight programs and leading significant software testing initiatives. My technical toolkit is comprehensive, including proficiency in Java, Python, C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a variety of frameworks and technologies.

In every role, I've thrived in Agile environments, eagerly taken on additional responsibilities, and enjoyed collaborating with cross-functional teams. My commitment to continuous learning and improvement is reflected in my proactive approach to acquiring new skills, like completing the C3.AI Software Developer Track.
